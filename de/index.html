Vision

The modules of this project should support you to transform your swimming pool into a smart swimming pool:

  • Automation of the ciculation time for cleaning water
  • Manage water heating by additional pump for heating (solar) circuit
  • Independentancy of specific smarthome servers
  • Open Source (MIT License)
  • Open for suggestions and improvements
  • Supporting standards
  • Working without WiFi connection
  • Can be operated via smartphone
  • Can be operated via hardware
  • Easy to setup
  • Modular to extend
